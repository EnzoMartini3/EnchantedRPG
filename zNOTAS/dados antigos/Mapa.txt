-----------------------Relevant-Places------------------------
-Lime Plaza:Heal your ______,talk with people and help citizens in need.
All in a beautiful garden.Can be found on every town.
-Lure Fields:Some places have a natural luring aroma,so the luring effect
will be applied to you.
-Aura Fields:There are specific places that have powerful auras.
There are 6 different types of aura that gives you boosts and 
causes certain species to evolve(Absolute Aura will always trigger this Growth).
.Nature Aura:Increases max sp by 25%(Your ______ feel refreshed)
.Thunder Aura:Increases offensive stats by 30%(Your ______ feel energized)
.Snowy Aura:Buffs and debuffs will last 2 extra turns(Your ______ feel relaxed)
.Distortion Aura:Nullifies weaknesses and resistences(Your ______ feel strange)
.Twilight Aura:Decreases Mizule cost by 25%(Your ______ feel calm)
.Absolute Aura:Increases xp gain by 50%(Your ______ feel powerful!)
-Tourism Areas:The most beautiful places in Haveros.You should
take a look at the Sacred Shore,it's unbelievable!You can even
place a warp there,so you can enjoy the view it whenever you want.
-Bakery:A small store that can be found in Bripbale Town.You can buy
bread and treats for your partners,with a special discount!.
-Akairis Resort:A little hotel,where you can rent a room and relax
a little after a tiring day.
-Gale's House:Found on Scarlet Valley,Gale will ask you if you want to learn how to fly.
They need to be on PL 20 or more to fly,and your score is measured in stars.
-Summoning Circle:Can be found on .You can sacrifice the some items
to summon Phantad or get other items in exchange.If you sacrifice items
5 times in a row and don't get anything in exchange,Darbyss will appear.
-Enjoeneer's House:Can be found in Faborem Town.He will upgrade one
of your tools if pay him.But he only accepts payment in fluffy balls!
-Arcade:Play videogames,inside a videogame!Play Bouncy Adventures(Springi),Melody
Mixer(Deedjay) and Snowdrift(Shivern)
